About Us

UKG (Ultimate Kronos Group) is an American multinational technology company that provides human resource (HR) and workforce management services. UKG's cloud-based HCM (human capital management) solutions help organizations anticipate and adapt to their employees' needs, including HR, payroll, time, and culture. UKG's services include analytics to help leaders make decisions, actionable data to understand employee performance, and AI-backed insights to anticipate workforce needs.
